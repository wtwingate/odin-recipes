Poppy Seed Chicken

Description

This one is the very definition of comfort food and a perennial favorite at the Wingate household. The contrast between the creamy chicken casserole and the crunchy Ritz topping is incredible. Serve over rice or pasta, or just eat it by the spoonful!

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken
  • 1 can of cream of chicken soup
  • 1/2 pint of sour cream
  • 1 1/2 sticks of butter
  • 2 rolls of Ritz crackers
  • 1 tablespoon of poppy seeds

Instructions

  1. Roast chicken, remove bones, and chop up the meat.
  2. Place chopped chicken in a greased baking pan.
  3. Mix sour cream and soup and pour over chicken; spread evenly across the baking pan.
  4. Mix crushed Ritz crackers, poppy seeds, and melted butter together in a bowl.
  5. Evenly spread mix across the surface of the casserole.
  6. Bake at 400 degrees for 30 - 40 minutes, until casserole is bubbling and top is golden brown.
  7. Let cool at room temperature for 15 minutes, then enjoy!

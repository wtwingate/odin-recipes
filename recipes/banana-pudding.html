Banana Pudding

Description

This recipe will make a deep dish full of the sweetest southern comfort food you've ever had the joy of tasting. Vanilla pudding, wafers, bananas, and whipped cream are layered together like some sort of heavenly lasagna. Perfect for all times and seasons, bring this out to the next potluck and watch your social credit soar to new heights.

Ingredients

  • vanilla pudding
  • vanilla wafers
  • bunch of bananas
  • whipped cream

Instructions

  1. Make some vanilla pudding. You can use store-bought instant pudding, but your taste buds will thank you later for the modest effort it takes to make it from scratch.
  2. Take a large dish or serving bowl, and layer the bottom with vanilla wafers.
  3. Spread a layer of vanilla pudding evenly on top of the wafers.
  4. Lay a single layer of sliced bananas on top of the pudding.
  5. Repeat steps 2 through 4 until the dish is full.
  6. Let cool in refrigerator for at least 2 hours before serving.
  7. Immediately before serving, add a layer of fresh whipped cream on top.
